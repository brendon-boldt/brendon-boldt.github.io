‚òï Coffee Brewing Notes

  • Use the water jugs for the percolators, not the tap water.
  • 1 cup of grounds per 16 cups of water (if you fear arithmetic: 1.5/24, 2/32, 2.5/40, 3/48, 3.5/56, 4/64).
  • Do not put more than two percolators on a given (set of) outlet; the current draw could trip a breaker (so I've heard).
  • If you get the coffee brewing by 8am, it will be a lot stronger.
  • To clean the percolators, rinse them out with water and scrub any remaining visible residue, if necessary.
  • The percolator with plastic the faux-crystal top should not be filled with more than 40 cups; it might not fully brew the grounds otherwise.
